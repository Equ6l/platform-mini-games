Tic Tac Toe

Also known as Noughts and Crosses, Tic Tac Toe is a simple yet enjoyable board game for two players. The rules : three of O or X symbols in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Rock Paper Scissors

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple and fun hand game played by two people. The rules : rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock. If both players show the same hand sign, it's a tie and the game is repeated. Have fun !
